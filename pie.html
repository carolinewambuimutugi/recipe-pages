Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie

INGREDIENTS

The following are ingredients to use in your recipe

  1. 1 cup white sugar
  2. 2 tbsp all pupose flour
  3. 3 tbsp cornstarch
  4. 1/4 tsp salt
  5. 1 1/2 cups water
  6. 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  7. 2 tbsp butter
  8. 4 eggs yolk beaten
  9. 1(9inch) pie crust,baked
  10. 4 egg whites
  11. 6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

The user shoukd use the following directions

  1. Preheat Oven:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C).
  2. Make Lemon Filling:In a medium saucepan
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt
    • Stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
    • Cook over medium high heat,stirring frequentlty,until kixture comes to a boil
    • Stir in butter
    • Place egg yolks in a samll bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
    • Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar nixture
    • Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring contantly until thick
    • Remove from heat
    • Pour fillingd into baked pastry shell
  3. Make Meringue:In a large glass orr meatl bowl...
    1. Whip egg whites until foamy
    2. Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    3. Spread meingue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake:Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or untill meringue is golden brown
